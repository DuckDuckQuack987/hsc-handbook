Time Management Tips

Pomodoro Technique

Study for 25 mins, then take a 5 min break. After 4 cycles, take a longer 15-30 min break.

Time Blocking

Assign specific blocks of time for each subject/activity in your calendar.

Energy Matching

Schedule difficult tasks when you're most alert (morning/evening person?).

Buffer Time

Include 15-30 min buffers between tasks for transitions and unexpected delays.

Study Technique Tips

The Feynman Technique

  1. Choose a concept to learn
  2. Explain it in simple terms
  3. Identify gaps in your explanation
  4. Review and simplify further

Interleaving

Mix different subjects or topics during study sessions rather than focusing on one thing at a time. This improves retention and problem-solving skills.

Elaboration

Connect new information to what you already know. Ask "how" and "why" questions to deepen understanding.
